About RevelPlant

RevelPlant is an AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize plant care through intelligent diagnostics and comprehensive guidance.

Our mission is to make plant identification and disease detection accessible to everyone, from casual plant lovers to professional gardeners. We combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with user-friendly interface to provide instant, accurate plant diagnostics.
